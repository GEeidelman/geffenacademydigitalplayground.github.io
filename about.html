About Us



Mission Statement:

We want to unite ideas and reality together. In order to do this we need to accomplish goals to keep our collective stable.
  • Provide an area for our members to open up.
  • Provide adequate resources needed for the functioning of the club.
  • Be there for others when they are stuck.
  • Let our members express their creativity.
  • Set an example of the standard that we hold our club to.
If we don't meet all of these standards our club will be considered a failure on every level.
